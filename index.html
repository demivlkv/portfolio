01. About Me

Hello! My name is Demi Hayashi. My passion for coding all began back in the early 2000s, when Geocities and MySpace was a trend. I remember customizing my MySpace profile page to look unique compared to my peers', and I was designing & coding websites from scratch HTML and CSS. After graduating the University of Florida with a Fine Arts degree, I worked in sales and as a flight attendant. Throughout the years, I have worked on many small projects. My love for coding and web design is still very strong so I hope to be able to learn more throughout this coding journey.
